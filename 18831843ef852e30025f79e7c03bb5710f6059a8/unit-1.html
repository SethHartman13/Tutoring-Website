Unit 1

Unit 1 covers two main topics: Unit Conversion and Vector Operations

Unit Conversions are important as in a world with different measurements, engineers need to know how to work with all units while adapting units to the area that they are working in. HW 1.1 teaches the concept of cross-converting.

Vector Operations helps us engineers understand how vectors work. While in HW 1.2 we learn how to add vectors through geometric means. HW 1.3 teaches us how to break apart the components of a vector, add them, then reform the resultant vector.
